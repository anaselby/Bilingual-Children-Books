To my mother, I will always love you!

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

You want to know something about my dads workers? Theyre saying that I look like dad, but I know I dont, I look just like you, I said to my mom while working on our delicious cake!


I really like talking to you I said to my mom. There are many reasons why I want to be like you: youre smart, youre a hard worker and you know how to bake!.

 

 

Remember when we were at the park and everyone was enjoying your treats?

It was epic!



And when we all decided to be brave and ride the scariest ride in the park? You said Its going to be fun! Go right ahead! You gave us strength with your words and we all went up. I love my mom and my friends, theyre so intrepid!

 

 

My dad is cool too, but he never will be cooler than you, mom. I love my dad, but there is more to be improved. I said.

 

 

 

Every summer you would take me to the country area to visit our family.

AHHHHH! there is a bunch of fireflies I yelled.

I still remember how you always have the right way to calm down my fears.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

And the monkeys looked at us with attention, they were jealous of our friendship!


So many memories, so much love! When they came to tell me about the accident, a part of me left my body forever.


They were talking to me but I couldnt hear them. Their voices sounded far and their faces looked blurry. There were many flowers saying goodbye to you.

 

My mom is out there, somewhere. She will never be gone forever! I really, really miss her so much! But I know, Ill be there soon.

The End
